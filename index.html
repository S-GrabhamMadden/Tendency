A small apartment near enough to the center of the city, and the lifeline of steady if altogether unfulfilling employment. You live a life conventional, unexceptional. On rare nights of stillness, your dreams are anything but; compelling and authoritative visions of a past never lived, or a future unobtainable, conveyed in rich metaphor. A new dawn awaits you tomorrow, soon a new year, even a new President if such things were of interest to you. But tonight, it is still, and you dream of golden eggs and unfamiliar cities.

Dull, sorrowful.
